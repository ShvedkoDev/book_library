Abstract

This book introduces Kosrae, an island in the Federated States of Micronesia, through a concise description of its geography, culture, local customs, and natural resources. Notes

Does not conform to standard Chuukese orthography [lacks diacritics]. Yapese translation of the original story written in Woleaian [Geeshim Woongi]. Also available in Ulithian, Satawalese, and English book versions.
