REFERENCES AND RECOMMENDED READINGS

The references below offer a sample of writing on various educational practices and strategies. The primary references are the FSM Language Policy and the FSM Vernacular Language Arts Student Learning Outcomes. These two documents form the foundation to which this Resource Guide is aligned and which it is designed to support and strengthen. Users are encouraged to seek additional references for use in their classrooms and communities and to explore the many materials available in the online Resource Library, where relevant resources are organized under the “Educator Support” category.
