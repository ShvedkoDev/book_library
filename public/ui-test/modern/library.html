Marine Ecosystems of Micronesia

2022 Pacific Education Press Hardcover in English

A comprehensive guide to marine ecosystems throughout Micronesia, covering biodiversity, conservation efforts, and traditional knowledge systems. Includes detailed photographs and case studies from the region.

Applied Mathematics for Island Communities

2023 Micronesian Education Center Digital PDF in English

Interactive lesson plans that connect mathematical concepts to daily life in Pacific Island communities. Focuses on practical applications in navigation, agriculture, and resource management.

Climate Change and Our Islands

2021 Pacific Climate Institute Video Series in English

Age-appropriate video series explaining climate change impacts on Pacific Islands. Features local scientists and community leaders discussing adaptation strategies and environmental protection.

Pacific Island Literature and Writing

2020 Island Heritage Publications Paperback in English/Chuukese

Collection of contemporary and traditional Pacific Island literature with writing exercises. Bilingual text supports language preservation while developing English literacy skills.

Traditional Navigation Methods of Micronesia

2019 Pacific Maritime Research Academic Journal in English

Research paper documenting traditional navigation techniques used by Micronesian seafarers. Includes interviews with master navigators and analysis of wayfinding methods passed down through generations.

About the Library

The Micronesian Teachers Digital Library serves educators and students across the Pacific Islands with high-quality educational resources.

Our mission is to support excellence in education through accessible, culturally relevant digital content.
