2. Acceptable Use

  • Materials are for educational and non-commercial use only
  • Content may be used for teaching, learning, and research purposes
  • Users must respect copyright and intellectual property rights

Your privacy is important to us. We collect and use personal information only as necessary to provide library services and improve user experience.
